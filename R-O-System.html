R.O System

R.O Working

Step 1: Pre-Filtration

1. Raw water enters the system through the inlet valve.
2. The pre-filter (usually a sediment filter) captures larger particles, dust, and debris.

Step 2: Reverse Osmosis

1. The pre-filtered water passes through the R.O membrane.
2. The semi-permeable membrane filters out impurities, bacteria, viruses, and dissolved solids.
3. Impurities are rejected and flushed away.

Step 3: Post-Filtration

1. The purified water passes through the post-filter (usually an activated carbon filter).
2. The post-filter removes any remaining impurities, improves taste, and odor.

Step 4: Storage

1. The purified water is stored in a tank for convenient access.

Step 5: Dispensing

1. When the faucet is opened, purified water flows from the tank.
