Working Aatamaker

An atta maker grinds whole grains like wheat into flour. The powerful motor drives grinding stones or blades, crushing the grains into fine powder. The fresh flour is collected in a container, ready for use in cooking. It's efficient, quick, and ensures the flour retains its nutritional value.
